Spaghetti and Meatballs Recipe

An amazing and simple Spaghetti and Meatballs Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. spaghetti pasta or favorite pasta shape
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 Pinch crushed red pepper flakes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 cup water or red wine
  • 1 (28-ounce) can of crushed tomatoes
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese

Recipe Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium-high heat (we use a Dutch oven). Add the shaped meatballs and cook until browned, about 8 minutes. As the meat cooks, use a wooden spoon to break it up.
  2. Add the onions and cook, stirring every once and a while, until softened and browned, about 4-5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the garlic, tomato paste, oregano, and red pepper flakes and cook, stirring continuously for about 1 minute.
  4. Pour in the water and use a wooden spoon to scrape up any bits of meat or onion stuck to the bottom of the pot.
  5. Stir in the tomatoes, 3/4 teaspoon of salt, and a generous pinch of black pepper. Bring the sauce to a low simmer. Cook, uncovered, at a low simmer for 25 minutes.
  6. About 15 minutes before the sauce finishes cooking, bring a large pot of salted water to the boil, and then cook pasta according to package directions. Check every few minutes for doneness
  7. Take the sauce off of the heat, and then stir in the basil.
  8. Toss in the cooked pasta, and then leave for a minute so that the pasta absorbs some of the sauce. Toss again, and then serve with parmesan sprinkled on top.
  9. Enjoy your freshly cooked spaghetti dish!
